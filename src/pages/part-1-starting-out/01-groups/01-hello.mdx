<Callout kind="definition" title="Group">
A *group* is a pair $(G, \star)$ consisting of a set $G$ and a binary
operation $\star \colon G \times G \to G$ that has an identity, has inverses,
and is associative. The integers $\ZZ$, the rationals $\QQ$, and the cyclic
group $\Zc{n}$ are all groups under their natural operations.
</Callout>
