The prototype example:

<Callout kind="example" title="Dot product on ℝⁿ">
Let $e_1, \dots, e_n$ be the standard basis of <Term k="RRn">$\RR^n$</Term>. Define

$$\langle a_1 e_1 + \dots + a_n e_n,\; b_1 e_1 + \dots + b_n e_n \rangle \defeq a_1 b_1 + \dots + a_n b_n.$$

This is the usual dot product. It is symmetric and bilinear by inspection. To check <Term k="positiveDefinite">positive definiteness</Term>: $\langle v, v \rangle = a_1^2 + \dots + a_n^2$, which is zero exactly when every $a_i = 0$.
</Callout>
